I'm a Mega Jaguar?! How did that happen?!

It all started when my super-cool super-smart parents experimented on me before I was born. They're scientists from a burrow of scientists (that's what we called our underground homes, by the way—burrows) who were try to find a way to demute-ify the surface world. Their boss, Emelia, did not like mutes (mutants, you know, like talking animals, giant animals, animals with extra eyes or legs) and wanted to use their research to take away their mute abilities. She was awful. But thankfully, my parents were able to see how wrong she was—through me.
